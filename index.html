About Me

Currently, I am a second-year Master of Computer Science student at Stanford University, working in the CogAI Lab under the supervision of Prof. Jiajun Wu, and mentored by Hong-Xing Yu. I am applying for PhD programs in Computer Science for Fall 2026.

Research Focus. My current research focuses on 3D Vision and Generative AI. My past work covers 3D scene reconstruction, physical dynamics simulation and real-time video generation models. In a longer term, I am interested in building more general and robust machines that can understand and interact with the dynamic physical world.
